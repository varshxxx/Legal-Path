Bail Petition

A bail petition is a formal request submitted to a court by an accused individual or their legal representative, seeking temporary release from custody while their case is under investigation or trial. It highlights the applicant's willingness to comply with legal procedures, assures attendance at all court hearings, and emphasizes reasons such as health issues, family responsibilities, or lack of prior offenses to justify the request.
